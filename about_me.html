This is my About Me Page!


I am excited to be a part of the Panther family at CE King High School for the 2019-2020 school year. This is my fourth year at C.E. King and my third year teaching Computer Science. I obtained a B.S. in Business Administration from Colorado Christian University and a Master's from Liberty University. I am currently working towards a PhD in Educational Leadership at Piedmont International University. I am certified to teach Math (7-12), ESL, and Computer Science (8-12). I am certified to teach Project Lead the Way Computer Science Principles and Computer Science Essentials.
